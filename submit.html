Submitting
Share an essay on politics, culture, and art! Heart Cellar reviews all submissions from writers of color and does not discriminate against sexual orientation, religion, or gender identity.

Send an email to submission@heartcellar.com with an attached PDF of the submission piece with the following heading:

“Name - Title of Submission Piece - Racial Identity”

Essays must be within a 1,000 to 7,500-word limit with a list of all works cited and may include footnotes, figures, and illustrations.
